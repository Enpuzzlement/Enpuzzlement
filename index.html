ATTENTION ALL PARTNERS:

Enpuzzlement is accepting shoppers by appointment only and online order pickup.

While our online inventory website is under development here is the process to find our inventory.


1.     Join Enpuzzlement Up & Coming private group.

2.    Once in the group you can go to "Media" then "Albums" to view by category.

3.    Email us at enpuzzlement2009@gmail.com with the product names you want to order and then quantity.


We will respond if we have any questions or are out of stock of any items. You can then donate via the gateway or in person when you pickup your items.
