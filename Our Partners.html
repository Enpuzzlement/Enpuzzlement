Nightingales Harvest

Nightingales Harvest is a food and toiletry pantry established on May 27, 2011 with over 35 hundred families with a ratio of 3 family members per household. We feel Nightingales Harvest is unique because you always have a choice and you never just come and pick up your box. Nightingales Harvest has a partnership with Toledo organizational grows community garden. Nightingale Harvest also has its own cancer community garden on site where patients can pick fresh produce during prime seasons.
